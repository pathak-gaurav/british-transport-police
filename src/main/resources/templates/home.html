Convicted for stealing newspapers in London

Two men have been convicted of stealing free newspapers at London Bridge station in what is believed to be the first ruling of its kind. Jhon Mejia, 32 and Marcel Javier Larez-Serrano, 37, were spotted loading batches of free papers into the rear of a van on the evening of Thursday 6 April 2017.

Convicted after kicking a seagull to death

A man who stamped a seagull to death in Holyhead train station has been convicted under the Animal Cruelty Act.Martin Maguire, 52, and of no fixed address, was found guilty of intentionally killing a wild bird at Caernarfon Magistrates’ Court on 4 February, following the shocking and brutal incident last year.
